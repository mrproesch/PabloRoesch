4. EV Expansion & BESS Dependency

The expansion of EVs in regions with unstable grids relies heavily on decentralized energy. Portable BESS units do not just power the bike; they are charged via off-grid solar micro-stations and serve as home energy backups, fundamentally changing the energy distribution model.
