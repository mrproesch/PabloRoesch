Parasitic Load Composition

A detailed look at the 5.4% auxiliary load. Active thermal management (HVAC) is critical for maintaining lithium-ion cells within their optimal 20°C to 30°C temperature window, making it the dominant consumer.

Dynamic Efficiency vs. Power Intensity (C-Rate)

System efficiency is not static. As the speed of charge or discharge (C-Rate) increases, internal electrochemical resistance grows exponentially, generating excess heat and requiring heavier HVAC operation.
